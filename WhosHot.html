No One.


As the familiar opening screen of Call of Duty flashed across their eyes, The Bounty-Boys, a once-formidable squad
well-known for their tactical prowess and unwavering determination, could not help but grin with anticipation.
It had been some time since they had graced the virtual battlefields, and they were eager to prove that their skills remained sharp.

However, as they embarked on their first match, reality swiftly shattered their illusions.
The controls felt foreign, their aim was off, and their teamwork seemed to have evaporated.
The enemy team, sensing their vulnerability, relentlessly attacked, leaving The Bounty-Boys reeling in their wake.
Despite their desperate attempts to regain their former glory, they found themselves outmaneuvered and outgunned at every turn.

Kills were scarce, and deaths came in droves.
The once-unstoppable force had become a mere shadow of their former selves, unable to secure a single victory.
With each defeat, their frustration and disappointment grew, turning the once-joyous reunion into a sobering reminder of the unforgiving nature of time.


But all is not lost.

This team has proven their ability to bounce back from tough nights.
Their next session will be highly anticipated, as they will hope to prove that they are still a force to be reckoned with.
